Silky Singh

I am a Machine Learning Research Associate at MDSR Lab, Adobe. I have explored image editing, self-supervised learning, image segmentation and text-conditioned style transfer, developing a broad interest in Computer Vision and Machine Learning.

In 2022, I graduated from IIT Delhi with a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Computing. My thesis project on "Deep Neural Network approximation for image denoising" was advised by Prof. Sivananthan Sampath. During my undergrad, I spent wonderful time at Adobe Research working with Dr. Kuldeep Kulkarni on context-aware object insertion in images, and MPI-SWS working on program synthesis with Prof. Eva Darulova.
